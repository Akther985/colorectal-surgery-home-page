Our team has over 75 years of general surgery experience and consistently upholds our reputation for excellence and satisfaction. Feel the difference—at Sulphur Surgical Clinic!

Dr. Devin Seale

Dr. Devin Seale is a native of Sulphur, LA. He is the son of A. Kent Seale, one of the founding partners of Sulphur Surgical Clinic. Dr. Devin Seale attended Washington and Lee University for his undergraduate education. Dr. Devin Seale earned his medical degree from Tulane School of Medicine in New Orleans. He completed his General Surgery Residency at Ochsner Medical Center in New Orleans. He currently lives in Lake Charles with his wife and two children.

Dr. Walter P. Ledet, Jr. is a board-certified general surgeon. He is a native of Natchitoches, LA, and a Northwestern State University graduate. He received his medical degree from LSU School of Medicine in New Orleans and completed his general surgery residency at Confederate Memorial Medical Center in Shreveport, LA. Dr. Ledet served two years as a staff surgeon at the U.S. Navy Hospital in Lemoore, CA. Dr. Ledet moved to Sulphur, LA joining Dr. A Kent Seale. Together, they founded Sulphur Surgical Clinic which provides surgical care for the residents of Sulphur and surrounding areas.
